💳 Payment Policy

Simple structure: 50% advance to begin, 50% upon delivery. International bank transfer, Wise, or PayPal. Invoices provided for all transactions.

Payment Structure

  • 50% advance payment required before work commences on any project.
  • Remaining 50% payable upon delivery of the completed dashboard.
  • Monthly retainer billed at the start of each month, in full, in advance.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Xflow — cross-border payments, preferred for UAE clients.
  • Wise (multi-currency) — fast international transfers with low fees.
  • PayPal — business invoice option available.
  • Bank transfer (SWIFT / NEFT) — details shared on request.

Refund Policy

  • Advance payment (50%) is non-refundable once work has commenced.
  • If the project is cancelled before work begins, the full advance is refunded within 3 business days.
  • No refund is applicable once the final dashboard has been delivered and approved.
  • Additional revisions beyond plan limits are billed at AED 149 per revision.

Retainer Cancellation

  • 2 business days' notice required to pause or cancel a monthly retainer.
  • No partial refunds for mid-month cancellations.

📋 Terms & Conditions

By engaging Om Bagal Analytics, you agree to these terms. They are designed to protect both parties and ensure a clear, professional working relationship.

Scope of Work

  • Each project scope is agreed upon in writing (WhatsApp or email) before work begins.

  • Dashboards are delivered as interactive HTML files, Power BI reports, or hosted links.

Revisions

  • Revisions requested more than 7 days after delivery may be treated as new work.

Intellectual Property
